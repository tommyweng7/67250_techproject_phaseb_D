Welcome to Monomuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring where art and technology meet. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, the museum highlights how digital innovation is reshaping the way we create and experience art. Whether you’re visiting for the first time or returning as a member, MonoMuse offers fresh perspectives, hands-on experiences, and engaging events that encourage curiosity and creativity while examining the role of technology in culture and everyday life.
