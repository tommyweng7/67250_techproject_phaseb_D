Current Exhibitions

Clockwork Dreams: The Art of Automata

Step into the intricate world of Victorian-era automata, where precision mechanics meet imagination. This exhibition highlights the craftsmanship of horologists who brought these mechanical marvels to life.

Pixels in Motion: Interactive Digital Art

Experience art that reacts to you. From motion sensors to interactive screens, this exhibition explores how technology transforms the way we engage with digital creations.

AI and the Future of Creativity

Discover the intersection of artificial intelligence and artistic expression. This exhibition presents works generated with AI tools, questioning the boundaries between human and machine creativity.
